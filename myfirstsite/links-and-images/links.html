This example is about links and images.

This particular page is about links! There are three kinds of links:

  • Absolute links, are like Mozilla Developer Network, where we are directing users to a different website.
  • Relative links, are like Extras, where we are directing users to a page within our own website.
  • Root-Relative links, are like Home Page, which is the homepage of our website, but we can't use it right now.
